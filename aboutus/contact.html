Set-up in 2015, the ALS Care & Support group is a 24 x 7 assistance community, which continue to touch the lives of hundreds of patients and their caregivers of India since inception. Functioning more as a close family, it guides fellow PALS/CALS with each and every aspect of the disease based on their own experiences and extensive research. Be it nutrition, PEG management, using BiPAP, home ICU management, creating ALS awareness, patient advocacy and much more. ALS Care & Support Foundation got registered as NPO under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013 on 21st September 2022.
