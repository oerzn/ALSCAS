Supporting Items-

  1. Air Purifier
  2. Power plugs/extensions for equipment A/C power supply for easy access during procedures.
  3. TV – entertainment – position it where the patient can watch it while lying down.
  4. AC – to maintain room temperature.
  5. Almirah – to organize medical supplies.
  6. Rattle/Bell for night call/emergencies.
  7. Tables – to keep ventilator, humidifier, suction machine, air purifier.
  8. Wall clock – helps organize procedures and feed timings (always in front, so mumma also sees).
  9. IV stand or creative wall socket for IV holding.
  10. Cushions/pillows and extra shawl for preparing side support.
  11. Mini hammer (hamam dasta handle) for crushing medicines – use a normal steel bowl for better hygiene.
  12. Polythene sheets (2 ft × 3 ft) to prevent urine leakage at night with diaper use (diaper pads are now readily available). Fold edges downward to avoid direct contact with synthetic material.
  13. Blender – for PEG feed preparation.
  14. Chhanni – to sieve the final feed solution.
  15. 4 soft cotton cloths (dupattas) to place below exhalation valve to stop water droplets from reaching the patient's body.
  16. Lightweight, very soft blankets for winter (as a heater may be used intermittently).
  17. Urine plastic pan.
  18. Dettol hand wash liquid.
